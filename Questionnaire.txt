4. What is the difference between relative positioning and absolute positioning in HTML?

Answer:   Relative Positioning -  setting the top , right , bottom and left property of an element. It will adjust from its normal position .
Syntax: position : relative;

Absolute positioning – an element with absolute positioning will cause it to adjust its position with respect to its parent 
Syntax: position : absolute;
